About the Founder

Shyam Sundar R is the Founder & CEO of ScholarVault — an academic technology initiative focused on creating a trustworthy and modern ecosystem for researchers, faculty members, students, institutions, and conference communities.

His professional journey began in customer support and conference operations, where he gained hands-on experience working with research conferences, academic communities, and institutional stakeholders. These experiences revealed significant challenges within the research ecosystem — including fragmented resources, predatory conferences, limited visibility for scholars, and disconnected academic networks.

To address these challenges, he founded ScholarVault on 26 February 2026 with a vision of building a trusted digital infrastructure that empowers academic growth, research collaboration, scholarly visibility, and conference transparency.

Extended Bio

Shyam Sundar R is the Founder & CEO of ScholarVault, an academic technology platform focused on creating the most trusted research ecosystem in India. ScholarVault connects researchers, institutions, conferences, publishers, and academic communities through technology — simplifying academic discovery, conference participation, scholarly networking, and professional visibility.

With over six years of professional experience spanning customer support, conference operations, and academic community engagement, Shyam identified critical pain points across the research landscape. Researchers routinely face fragmented resources, predatory conferences, opaque academic processes, and limited mechanisms for building scholarly credibility. These observations directly informed the creation of ScholarVault.

Founded on 26 February 2026, ScholarVault has achieved significant milestones in a short period: launching a Progressive Web App for researchers, completing GST registration and business banking, securing MSME/Udyam registration, and registering under the Startup India initiative. The platform has onboarded over 100 researchers and launched ICAHCR 2026, its first academic conference.

A key innovation is ScholarVault Verified — a conference evaluation and trust framework that helps researchers assess transparency, credibility, and publicly available academic indicators before participating in conferences and publication opportunities. This framework includes Conference Trust Scoring, Conference Risk Assessment, Indexing Verification, and Peer-Review Practice Assessment.

ScholarVault's vision is to become the digital backbone of global scholarly collaboration — enabling every researcher to discover opportunities, build credibility, share knowledge, and advance innovation without barriers.
